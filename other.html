Music

I'm a jazz trumpet player and sometimes play around the Boston area. I can sometimes be found at jam sessions including the Acton Jazz Cafe on Sundays, Devlin's in Brighton Center on Wednesday nights, and Lilypad in Cambridge on odd Sundays. I play with the American Symphony of Soul, a Funk/Soul/R&B group. I'm also part of the Citizen's Orchestra, a free jazz/unstructured improvisation group which plays at Outpost 186 and Lilypad.
